Untitled

by Anonymous

I lie awake at night
Thoughts clouded with disturbances
Everyone is asleep
Inner daemons thrust themselves against my rib cage
Trying to escape
Who am I not to let them?
I emerge slowly and acquire my empty canvas
Pure and untouched
Sitting there, waiting to be defiled
The emotions within are too powerful
They must be released
Each stroke of the pen is
Strong and firm
Pouring out the gooey innards of my heart
Primal, uncensored, real
Until the deed is done
No need to feel bad
I haven't hurt anyone
And as I examine my creation
My eyes fill with tears
I have taken something clean
And ruined it with human imperfection
So it joins the garbage among the others
As I reminder of what I want
But don't have
